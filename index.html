Emperor Charlemagne of The Holy Roman Empire

768 AD to 814 AD

Employment: Current ruler of the Holy Roman Empire

Current place of residence:  Aachen

Objective:   I embarked on a mission to unite all Germanic peoples into one kingdom, and convert the subjects to Christianity.

Skills:

  • Skilled military strategist/leader
  • Strong administrator

    • Education:

  • I was educated at the palace school primarily by Fulrad, the abbot of St. Denis.

    • Acheivements: 

  • United most of Western Europe for the first time since the Roman Empire

  • I was the first emperor of the Holy Roman Empire

  • I played a vital role in the spread of Christianity across Europe.
